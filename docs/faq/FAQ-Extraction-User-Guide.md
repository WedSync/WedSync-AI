## ðŸš¨ Troubleshooting

### "No FAQs Found"
**This means:** The system couldn't find FAQ content on your website

**Solutions:**
- âœ… Make sure you have a dedicated FAQ section on your website
- âœ… Try entering a direct link to your FAQ page (e.g., `yoursite.com/faq`)
- âœ… Check that your FAQs are formatted as questions and answers (not just paragraphs)
- âœ… Ensure your FAQ content is visible to visitors (not password protected)

### "Website Not Accessible"
**This means:** The system cannot reach your website

**Solutions:**
- âœ… Double-check the URL for typos
- âœ… Make sure your website is live and not under maintenance
- âœ… Verify your website uses HTTPS (secure connection)
- âœ… Test the URL in your browser first

### "Extraction Timeout" 
**This means:** Your website is taking too long to load

**Solutions:**
- âœ… Try again during off-peak hours
- âœ… Check if your website loads quickly in a browser
- âœ… Contact support if the issue persists

### "Low Confidence Categories"
**This means:** The AI isn't sure how to categorize some FAQs

**What to do:**
- âœ… Review these FAQs manually
- âœ… Choose appropriate categories yourself
- âœ… Consider editing the FAQ for clarity
